Buko Pearl Salad

Easy special dessert in one step!

Preparation Time: 1o mins.

Serving Size: 8


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose cream, chilled
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk, sweetened
  • 1 can DEL MONTE Fiesta Fruit Cocktail (432g), drained
  • 3/4 cup buko meat, shredded
  • 3/4 cup sago, cooked
  • 1 cup sago, big, cooked
  • 1/2 cup nata de coco, sweetened
  • 1/2 cup kaong, white

Instructions

  1. Combine all-purpose cream and condensed milk together in a bowl. Mix until well incorporated.
  2. Add DEL MONTE Fiesta Fruit Cocktail, buko, sago, nata, and kaong. Mix well then chill until ready to serve.
